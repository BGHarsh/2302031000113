What is
NEXUS '25?

NEXUS is the flagship annual techno-cultural festival of SVNIT, Surat — one of India's premier National Institutes of Technology. Each year, thousands of students from across the country converge here for three days of relentless innovation, creativity, and competition.

From cutting-edge hackathons and robotics challenges to electrifying music nights and theatrical performances — NEXUS has it all. It's more than a fest. It's a movement.

// 05 FAQ

Questions?
We Got You.

Is registration free? +
Yes! General registration for NEXUS '25 is completely free. Some premium workshops have a nominal fee of ₹99–₹199 for materials.
Can students from other colleges participate? +
Absolutely! NEXUS is open to students from all colleges across India. Just register on the website and show your valid college ID at the venue.
Is accommodation available? +
Yes, limited accommodation is available on campus at ₹200/night. Book during registration on a first-come, first-served basis.
How do I register for specific events? +
After completing general registration, you'll receive a unique participant ID. Use this to register for individual events through the NEXUS portal up to 2 hours before each event starts.
What are the prizes? +
The total prize pool is ₹5+ Lakhs across all events. Top events like Hackathon 2.0 carry a ₹1,00,000 first prize. All winners receive certificates and goodies.
